Nokia 3310

Nokia 3310 was my first phone. I had it when I was in university. It was a simple phone with the texting ability that was enticing. I enjoyed making ringtones using the ringtone composer. I also enjoyed playing Tetris on it.
In a way it marked the beginning of independence and so Nokia 3310 will always hold a special place in my memory.

Specs:

  • First released: 1 September 2000
  • Discontinued: 2002
  • Units sold: 126 million worldwide
  • Dimensions: 113 mm x 48 mm x 22 mm
  • Weight: 133 g
  • Display: 84 x 84 pixel pure monochrome
  • Sound: Monophonic ringtones
